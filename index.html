Dr. Martin Luther King Jr


He was an African American Baptist minister and activist who became the most visible spokesperson and leader in the Civil Rights Movement from 1955 until his assassination in 1968.



Dr King's Life


  • 1929 - He was born in Atlanta Georgia
  • 1951 - He attended Boston University
  • 1955 - He led the Montgomery bus boycott
  • 1964 - He won a Nobel Peace Prize for combating racial inequality through non-violent resistance
  • 1968 - He was assassinated
